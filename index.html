About Texas Pros Garage Doors

Founded in 2010, Texas Pros Garage Doors began with a simple mission: to provide San Antonio homeowners with reliable, professional garage door services. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in garage door installation and repair across the region, serving thousands of satisfied customers throughout our journey.

Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in delivering honest service, using quality materials, and ensuring every customer receives the attention they deserve. Our team of certified technicians stays current with the latest industry innovations, allowing us to offer cutting-edge solutions while maintaining our commitment to traditional service values.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of garage doors do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive selection including steel, wood, aluminum, and composite doors in various styles - traditional, contemporary, and custom designs. All our doors are available with different insulation options and finish choices.

What are your service hours and response times?

We provide 24/7 emergency service with typical response times under 2 hours. Regular service appointments are available Monday through Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM, with flexible scheduling to meet your needs.

What warranty coverage do you provide?

All new installations come with a comprehensive warranty covering parts and labor. We offer 1-year warranty on repairs and lifetime warranties on many installed components. Extended warranty options are also available.

How much does a typical garage door installation cost?

Installation costs vary based on door style, size, and features. Basic installations start at $800, while custom doors can range from $1,500-$5,000. We provide detailed quotes after an initial consultation to ensure transparency.

Do you offer maintenance programs?

Yes, we offer annual and semi-annual maintenance plans that include comprehensive inspections, adjustments, and lubrication. These programs help prevent costly repairs and extend your door's lifespan.
