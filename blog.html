Common Garage Door Problems and Solutions

Garage doors are crucial home components that often go unnoticed until problems arise. Common issues include doors failing to open or close due to dead batteries, misaligned sensors, or broken springs. Unusual noises during operation can signal loose hardware, lack of lubrication, or worn parts. While regular maintenance like visual inspections and lubrication can prevent many problems, some situations require professional intervention. These include broken springs, doors off tracks, and electrical issues. Understanding these common problems helps homeowners maintain their systems effectively and know when to seek expert help, ensuring safe and reliable operation.
